Introduction

Introduction

Hi there. Thanks for purchasing my template. I have spent about a year making and polishing this product. I tryed to incorporate best practices and solutions from top booking services like kayak, booking.com, tripadviser, airbnb, etc.

If you need support, don't hesitate to contact me on support@remtsoy.com. You should include purchase code in your letter.

Note that support is not designed to incorporate new features. If you have some ideas for new features, I am open to listen, but I am not guarantee that they will be implemented.

I know that the template is not perfect. And I have a long list of new features and bug fixes.

If there you can't navigate or search documentation, switch your browser to mozilla or safari or run documentation on any web server. More information here https://github.com/rstudio/bookdown/issues/353

You can also try online documentation http://remtsoy.com/tf_templates/tf-bookify-docs/_book/
